Facts About the Palace

People Involved In The Construction Of The Palace

The Palace of Versailles served as the center of power in France from the reign of Louis XIV until the French Revolution. Several people played important roles in the development of the palace including:

  • Louis Le Vau: Was the chief architect and played an important role in early stages of the palace's construction.
  • Andre Le Notre: Served as the landscape architect, working on the gardens of the palace.
  • Charles Le Brun: Served as the main painter and decorator, designing much of the palace's interior.

The Petit Trianon

Queen Marie Antoinette built a mini palace for herself called the Petit Trianon. She also built a mini French town around the palace which included facilities such as farms and servant quarters.
